Mission Statement

A space to promote and discuss reading at NCSSM

Book Club aims to celebrate and share a love of reading with NCSSM community, as educate the NCSSM community about literary topics pertinent to students today, such as censorship, information literacy, and diversity.

Books are a uniquely portable magic.
- Stephen King

What we do

Students studying information literacy.

Book club exists as a place for reading-inclined students at NCSSM to gather. Every month we host a group discussion on a new book in the library. We also host different reading related events throughout the year, such as National Banned Books Week, as well as promoting diversity in literature during cultural awareness periods such as Black History Month and Pride Month.
