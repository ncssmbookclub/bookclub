An Absolutely Remarkable Thing
Hank Green

NCSSM Book Club (students & faculty/staff) are reading "An Absolutely Remarkable Thing" by Hank Green - a super-fun, contemporary novel. Read this book over break, and then you can join in the discussion in the NCSSM Library on Wednesday, January 8, at 3:40pm (Meeting Block). All NCSSM Library copies are checked-out, but you can find this book, eBook, or eAudiobook at your local library or bookstore. Enjoy! There will also be tea and cookies!
